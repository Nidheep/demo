Karappuzha Dam

A perfect starter for a delicious visual treat of Wayanad. Simple but Strong, that makes Karapuzha one of the best sights here. The exuberant water, drenched hills, spectacular environment, and green islands make it a perfect spot for tourists from all around. Placed on the both sides of Karapuzha River, the dam is one of the largest earth dams of India. The best part of everything is that Caffeine Villa is just 12 km away from this eternal beauty.

Kuruvadweep

Kuruva Dweep is a preserved river delta, comprising a bundle of islands over the center of Kabini River in Wayanad. Spread over 950 acres of land, Kuruva Island is heavily occupied with rich flora and fauna. The geographical specifications of this island makes this place evergreen with a peacefully ambience. 13.3 km away from Caffeine Villa you could dwell deep into this packets of island.

Karlad Lake

The third largest fresh water lake in Kerala. Karlad Lake also hold the title of being the second largest in Wayanad. Ziplining, boating, rock climbing the list of adventure waiting for you out there goes on. Take a 14.6 km drive from the wonderful Caffeine Villa, you are there...!!!
