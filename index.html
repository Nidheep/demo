Homecoming Heritage

Do you wish to bring back those time you spent at your Tharavad ? Here is an excellent choice that would take you back to those grandma tales. To sleep hearing the lullabies by cuckoos and to wake up with the smell of fresh coffee, Caffeine Villa vows to amuse you with the WOW feeling. Delve into the 70 year old tradition clubbed with all the modern facilities you deserve. Enveloped by elegant coffee plantation, Caffeine Villa offers you the exact Wayanadan experience. Caffeine Villa is well maintained with complete care that makes you feel at home. A stay here would be a walk back to our childhood stories. Let’s welcome the Homecoming Heritage with Caffeine Villa.!!! A treasure nestled in the exquisite beauty of Wayanad, Caffeine Villa would please you with its lovely charm. Perched at the Wayanadan Hills, the 70 years old renovated tharavadu takes you to the ethnic and soothing experience of living within the deep coffee plantation.
